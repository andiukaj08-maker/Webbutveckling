Everything You Need to Know

Wide Model Range

From compact sedans like the A-Class and CLA, to flagship luxury with the S-Class, to rugged capability with the G-Class — there is a Mercedes-Benz for every lifestyle and every road.

Precision Engineering

Every bolt, every stitch, every line of code in your vehicle is engineered to exacting standards. Our AMG models are hand-built by a single engineer — one car, one person, total accountability.

Electric Future (EQ)

Our EQ lineup is leading the charge into a sustainable future. The EQS offers up to 770 km of range on a single charge — without sacrificing a single gram of the luxury you expect.

Safety First

Mercedes-Benz has pioneered automotive safety for decades — from the crumple zone to today's PRE-SAFE® system, which prepares the car and its occupants for an imminent collision automatically.

MBUX Technology

The Mercedes-Benz User Experience (MBUX) is an AI-powered infotainment system that learns your preferences, responds to your voice naturally, and turns every drive into a connected, personalized experience.

World-Class Service

With over 10,000 authorized service points in 170+ countries, wherever you are in the world, expert Mercedes-Benz technicians and genuine parts are never far away.
